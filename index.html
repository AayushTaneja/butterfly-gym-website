Cross-Fit

Cross fit is a high intensive training, with the combination of aerobic exercises, Olympic weightlifting and calisthenics for gaining strength, flexibility, power, speed, balance, coordination, accuracy, stamina and endurance. The components of cross fit are - warm-up, strength or skill, workout of the day and mobility session.

Kick-Boxing

The contact sport used for self-defence or general workout is the mixture of Karate and boxing is kickboxing. It builds the stamina, improve coordination and flexibility and burns calories with the fun workout. It also enhances agility and balance within the body for strengthen the muscles.

Weight Training

Building and maintaining muscle is necessary for all of us likewise weight training is the common type of strength training for developing the strength and size of skeletal muscles.
